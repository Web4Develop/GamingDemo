    We create games that we ourselves would play and enjoy.

    Game 4 Develop games are for player who enjoy single player games and immersion. The genre falls in the RPG & Action-Adventure, with open-world & sandbox being a common theme.

    Gaming List

    Releashed

    Medieval Kindoms

    Take control of your young Monarch and start learning how to maintain, expand or seperate your lands while figuring out who and why your parents was assassinated. And maybe discover they haven't finished with you or your family yet.

    Take control of your Monarch 'Sim' and live out your lives during the Medieval times. Eating, sleeping, socialising and training are needs for you to fulfill. As your Monarch Sim ages, you can marry and have children, so you can decide what skills for them to start learning. Once your Monarch Sim dies of old age or if you get captured and beheaded, your heir can not only take over, but could have a head start in leading the lands you left them.
